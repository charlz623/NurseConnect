🧑‍⚕️ Patient-Centered Tools

Patient-centered tools in nursing informatics help patients become more involved in their care, improving outcomes and satisfaction. These tools support transparency and health literacy.

  • Personal health portals for lab results and care plans.
  • Educational materials tailored to specific conditions.
  • Mobile apps for health tracking and medication reminders.

These features encourage patients to actively engage in their health journey, fostering collaboration with the care team.
