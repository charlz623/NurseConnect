Nursing informatics strengthens evidence-based practice (EBP) by ensuring that clinical decisions are supported with real-time, accurate data. It integrates research, clinical expertise, and patient values through tools like EHRs and clinical decision support systems.

  • Access to current clinical guidelines and best practices.
  • Data analytics to evaluate patient outcomes.
  • Decision support tools integrated in care workflows.

This approach ensures that nurses provide care that is safe, efficient, and aligned with the latest scientific findings.
